# ✦ Style Studio — Fashion Design

A friendly, browser-based fashion design studio. Pick a garment, dress the model,
play with colors and patterns, save your favorite looks, and export them as images.

**No install, no sign-up, works offline.** Everything is one file (`index.html`)
and all your saved looks live safely in your own browser.

## How to open it

- **Easiest:** double-click `index.html` — it opens in your web browser.
- **On a phone/tablet:** email yourself the file, or host it (see below), then open the link.
- Works in Chrome, Safari, Edge, and Firefox.

## What you can do

1. **Pick a garment** (left) — Dress, T‑Shirt, Hoodie, Skirt, or Pants, shown on a model.
2. **Change the style** — necklines, sleeve length, hemlines, fit, and more.
3. **Tap any part of the outfit** on the model to select it (or use the chips on the right).
4. **Color it** — choose from the palette or pick any custom color.
5. **Add a pattern** — stripes, polka dots, gingham, hearts, stars, daisies, and more,
   each with its own two colors.
6. **Style the model** — skin tone, hair color, and backdrop.
7. **♥ Save to Lookbook** — keep the look and reopen it later to tweak.
8. **⤓ Export PNG** — download a picture of the design to share or print.
9. **Undo** any change with the Undo button or `Ctrl/Cmd + Z`.

## Publishing it online (optional)

Because it's a single static file, you can host it anywhere that serves web pages
(GitHub Pages, Netlify, etc.). To use **GitHub Pages**: in this repository's
**Settings → Pages**, choose the branch and root folder, and GitHub gives you a link
you can open on any device.
